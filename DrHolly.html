Research Program

I am a "distributed" member of the RSRG-OhioState and RSRG-Clemson (RESOLVE/Reusable Software Research Group).

The work of the group deals with the disciplined engineering of component-based software systems and the software components (a.k.a. reusable software components) from which they can be built. The vision is to design and develop a language that supports carefully engineered component-based software, and a true verifying compiler for it. Our research language is called RESOLVE. The RESOLVE research effort is one of the longest running software engineering efforts in the USA. It spans foundational, practical, and educational aspects of software engineering and computing.

    • From ACM/IEEE Curricula 2001 we cover parts of: NC1, NC2, NC6, NC9, OS1, OS7, OS8, and OS12.
  • CSCI C251 Foundations of Digital Computing. Currently we are using the "Discrete Mathematics and its Applications" 7e, by Rosen.
    •  From ACM/IEEE Curricula 2001 we cover parts of: DS1 - DS5, and AL5
  • CSCI C343 (CS7) Data Structures. Currently we are using C++ and Microsoft's Visual Studio for this course.
    • From ACM/IEEE Curricula 2001 we cover parts of: PF3, PF4, PF5, AL1, AL2, PL5, PL6, PL9, SE1, SE6, SE9.
  • CSCI C455 Analysis of Algorithms.  Currently we are using the "Introduction to Algorithms" 3e, by Cormen.
    • From ACM/IEEE Curricula 2001 we cover parts of: AL1, AL2, AL6, DS5, PF2, SE10.
  • CSCI C490 Mobile Computing
    • MVC Design, GUI design, XML, JSON, handling user input, use of layouts and widgets, threading, cloud computing, design by contract and APIs
  • EDUC H427 Explorations on Diversity Education. Co-teach with Dr. Magdalena Herdoiza (School of Education).
    • Sponsored 5 CSCI/INFO students to study abroad in Quito Ecuador, June 2012.
    • Sponsored 2 CSCI/INFO students to study abroad in Quito Ecuador, June 2014.
  • INFO I308 Information Representation.  SQL query writing and basics of relational databases, and XML

My research background in component engineering heavily influences both of these courses.
